Course Overview

This qualification is ideal for individuals entering or currently working in adult care settings. It covers essential topics such as communication, safeguarding, infection control, and more.

Who is This Course For?

  • Newcomers to the adult care industry
  • Current care workers wanting formal recognition
  • Volunteers in care environments
  • Anyone preparing for Level 3 qualifications

Key Learning Areas

  • Communication in Care
  • Safeguarding & Protection
  • Health and Safety in Care
  • Duty of Care & Infection Control
  • Equality, Inclusion, and Diversity

Duration & Entry Requirements

Duration: 8–12 weeks (flexible)

Requirements: No formal qualifications. Basic English required.

Assessment & Certification

  • Portfolio of Evidence
  • Workplace Observations (if applicable)
  • Nationally Recognized Certificate by Highfield

Career Progression

  • Care Assistant
  • Support Worker
  • Further studies – Level 3 Diploma or Apprenticeships

Approved Qualifications We Deliver

  • Highfield Level 2 Certificate in Adult Social Care
  • Highfield Level 2 Award in Awareness of Dementia
  • Highfield Level 2 Award in End of Life Care
  • Highfield Level 3 Certificate in Health and Social Care
  • Highfield Level 2 and 3 Diplomas in Care
  • Highfield First Aid and Emergency First Aid at Work
  • Highfield Health and Safety Qualifications
  • Highfield Food Safety in Catering
  • Highfield Infection Prevention and Control
